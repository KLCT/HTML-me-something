The Journey Home


Detroit: The Birthplace

I was born in Detroit on June 7th, 1992. NASA's EUVE telescope was launched on this day. I too was launched into a new, cold, and unfamiliar world.

The EUVE


Memphis: The Shortstop

Due to an untimely fire, my family and I packed up and headed South. We stayed with my older sister in Elvis country. While living in Memphis, I learned all about the southern hospitality. In such a hot place, eveyone had a very warm and inviting disposition. In retrospect, the BBQ was 'OK', but more on BBQ related realizaions later.

Elvis country


Kansas City: Home

In 2004, my family decided to migrate back to the Midwest. We landed here in KC. KC has the best BBQ, in my opinion. I had BBQ as far north as New York, and no amount of sauce could disguise the bland flavorless pile. Memphis BBQ is tasty, and def worth trying on a trip, but not number 1. Maybe in the top 5 somewhere. I've had BBQ in Myrtle Beach, which was nothing to write home about. I won't detail my BBQ experience in Houston. There are so many options here in the city making a choice can be overwhelming! Here is my top 5 for some guidance:

  1. 1. (Oklahoma) Joe's
  2. 2. LC's
  3. 3. Big T's
  4. 4. Q 39
  5. 5. Gate's (an honorable mention of sorts)
